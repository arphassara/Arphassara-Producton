The shortcut

Short flim Project - Director, Scriptwriter & Editor

I wrote, directed, and edited the short film The Short Cut, inspired by real- life experiences. The story follows a young man seeking a shortcut to wealth by reviewing gambling websites, only to be deceived by a scam call center posing as police. Pla Too Siang Tai (The Fortune Mackerel)

Short Film Project (8th Short Film Production Workshop & Seminar) - Director & Editor

I directed and edited a short film as part of the 8th Short Film Production Workshop & Seminar, where the team represented Khon Kaen University among 21 universities nationwide and abroad. Directed and edited a short film about a grandfather and grandson who embark on an adventure following mysterious clues hidden inside a mackerel. Each clue leads them to new discoveries until the final revelation shows that it was all a surprise plan by the grandson for his grandfather. A light-hearted and heartwarming story that combines adventure with family bonding
